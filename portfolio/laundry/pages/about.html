Our Story

Founded in 2010, Fresh & Clean started as a small local laundry service with a simple mission: to make laundry day easier for busy families and professionals. What began as a single storefront has grown into a trusted name in professional laundry services across the region.

Over the years, we've served over 10,000 satisfied customers and completed more than 50,000 orders. Our commitment to quality, convenience, and customer satisfaction has remained unchanged throughout our journey.

Today, we combine traditional cleaning expertise with modern technology to deliver exceptional laundry services right to your doorstep. From eco-friendly detergents to real-time order tracking, we're continuously innovating to serve you better.
