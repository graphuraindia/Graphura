What Is Social Media Marketing (SMM)?

Social Media Marketing is the process of using platforms like Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter (X), YouTube, Pinterest and more to create brand awareness, engage your audience, and convert followers into loyal customers.

It helps your brand:

  • Stay visible in the minds of your customers
  • Build a strong brand identity
  • Attract high-quality leads
  • Maintain consistent market relevance
